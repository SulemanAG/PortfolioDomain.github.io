1. Predicting Optimal Tourist Routes (Varun T)

This initiative focuses on developing a system that leverages real-time data like traffic conditions, crowd density, and user preferences to recommend ideal travel routes for tourists.

Sustainable goals:

  • Distributes tourism traffic evenly to prevent overcrowding
  • Encourages the use of sustainable transport modes
  • Enhances accessibility to cultural and natural heritage sites

Challenges in implementation:

  • Integrating diverse data sources
  • Balancing system performance and accuracy
  • Ensuring privacy and security

2. Sorting Menu Items (Vishal H)

Sorting menu items efficiently is crucial for e-commerce and digital platforms in food and retail industries.

Sustainable goals:

  • Promotes informed consumer choices
  • Reduces decision-making time
  • Supports sustainable consumption patterns

Challenges in implementation:

  • Adapting to changes in popularity or pricing
  • Scaling efficiently for platforms
  • Addressing algorithm biases

3. Monitoring Traffic Flow (Saif Ali)

Managing traffic density at critical intersections is essential for promoting vehicle-free smart cities.

Sustainable goals:

  • Minimizes emissions
  • Supports active commuting methods
  • Enhances urban mobility

Challenges in implementation:

  • High dependency on IoT infrastructure
  • Real-time analysis of large datasets
  • Overcoming resistance from commuters

4. Optimizing Waste Management Routes (Suleman A)

Efficient waste collection is vital for urban hygiene and sustainability.

Sustainable goals:

  • Lowers fuel consumption
  • Reduces operational costs
  • Prevents missed waste pickups

Challenges in implementation:

  • Handling dynamic changes
  • Collecting and updating accurate data
  • Ensuring system compatibility

5. Monitoring Water Usage in Residential Areas (Varun & Vishal)

Water conservation is a critical concern in urban planning.

Sustainable goals:

  • Identifies wasteful water usage patterns
  • Promotes equitable water distribution
  • Aligns with SDG Goal 6

Challenges in implementation:

  • Collecting accurate data
  • Analyzing seasonal fluctuations
  • Encouraging residents to adopt practices

6. Optimizing City-Wide Internet Connectivity (Saif Ali)

As urban areas expand, internet connectivity becomes a key challenge.

Sustainable goals:

  • Supports digital inclusion
  • Reduces energy consumption
  • Improves remote work opportunities

Challenges in implementation:

  • Identifying optimal router placements
  • Managing the network infrastructure effectively
  • Addressing cybersecurity concerns
