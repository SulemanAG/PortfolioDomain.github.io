Projects Managed by Me

Optimizing City-Wide Internet Connectivity

This project focuses on optimizing the placement of Wi-Fi hotspots in a smart city using minimum spanning tree algorithms, ensuring efficient and equitable access to high-speed internet for all residents.

Optimizing Waste Management Routes

This initiative aims to minimize fuel consumption and operational costs in waste collection through optimal route planning using Dijkstra's algorithm. The goal is to reduce carbon emissions and improve the efficiency of waste management systems.
